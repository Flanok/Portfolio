Character Creator for D&D

front-end dev

In college, I worked on a project to create a randomly generated character sheet for Dungeons and Dragons. It seemed like it would be a challenge, and it was. The technology for Android Studio, Java, and being able to send PDFs through email was all very challenging and new. It was a lot of fun to work on and learn new things.

One of the students would have people over to play D&D every now and then, and sometimes they would be people who don't really play. His idea was to create these simple character sheets so they could start playing fast, and if they enjoyed it, clean up the character sheets before their next session.

This was also my first experience with Github - and while we didn't finish quite how we wanted to due to running out of time, we made considerable progress with how many new technologies we had to learn.
